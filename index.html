01 · ESTABLISHED 2011
About Mosaic Venture Lab

15 years building East Asia's deep-tech network — from the inside.

Mosaic Venture Lab is a Taipei-based investment and accelerator platform. We operate inside OEM programs, hospital networks, and government hubs — then invest in the companies we find there. Seven active portfolio companies. Three advisory exits. One fund, anchored by FSP Group.

15
01
2011 — 2018

Yushan Ventures

Founded in Taipei to bridge global automotive innovation with Asia's engineering capacity. The thesis: mobility, electronics, and biotech will be co-built between Western OEMs and East Asian manufacturers.

During this period we advised and supported fundraising for companies that went on to significant exits — Wikitude (AR SDK, acquired by Qualcomm 2021), Akonia Holographics (holographic optics, acquired by Apple 2018), and StradVision (ADAS perception, KOSDAQ IPO filing 2026). These were advisory relationships, not equity positions.

02
2019 — 2023

Mosaic Venture Lab

Became Yushan's deep-tech accelerator and investment arm. Built anchor programs with Audi Continental and LITEON — corporate programs that proved the operator model. Launched CellTech as the cell & gene therapy venture studio. Scaled to 20+ people across Taipei, San Francisco, and Germany.

MVL designed and launched the LITEON+ LITEON (semiconductor and sustainability) and Landseed Landseed (hospital-embedded medtech) programs — both continue independently. This track record of building and handing off programs is a capability, not a gap.

03
2024 — today

The platform now

Mosaic+ runs inside Taiwan Tech Arena — the government-backed hub under Taiwan NSTC. That is our primary accelerator. CellTech continues as our biotech venture studio.

The investment fund — FSP Mosaic Innovation & Sustainability L.P., anchored by FSP Group (TWSE: 3015) — launched in 2025 and backs seed-to-Series A deep tech companies across Taiwan, Japan, and Korea. Four sectors. Seven active portfolio companies. Defense tech engagements handled confidentially.

What sets us apart

Embedded operators,
not coordinators.

Most programs connect startups to networks. MVL embeds inside institutions — TTA, hospitals, OEM programs — and runs the actual operations. The difference shows up in pilot rates, procurement speed, and clinical validation timelines.

Our edge · 01
Embedded operators
We run programs inside OEMs and government hubs — not alongside them. Startups get real procurement conversations, not warm intros.
Our edge · 02
Hospital-validated medtech
Through the Landseed program we built, MVL has clinical validation infrastructure embedded inside a working hospital. Our biotech pipeline is built on that foundation.
Our edge · 03
FSP Group as strategic LP
A listed industrial anchor LP means portfolio companies get manufacturing access and supply-chain relationships on day one, not after they exit.
